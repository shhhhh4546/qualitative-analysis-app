# Sample Data Guide - Personas and Pain Points by Industry

This document outlines the personas and pain points captured in the sample data for Tulip Interfaces MES solution.

## Industry Analysis

### 1. **Automotive** (Plant Manager)
- **Pain Points**: Manual clipboard tracking, paper-based data entry, lack of real-time visibility, quality documentation on paper
- **Media**: Manufacturing Today magazine
- **Compelling Points**: Real-time dashboards, digitized processes

## Persona Patterns by Role

### **Plant Managers**
- Focus: Overall production efficiency, operator management
- Common Pain Points: Training, visibility, process standardization
- Industries: Automotive, Textiles, Metal Fabrication, Ceramics

### **Quality Managers**
- Focus: Compliance, documentation, traceability
- Common Pain Points: Paper-based systems, audit preparation, certification management
- Industries: Pharmaceutical, Packaging, Batteries

### **Operations Directors/VPs**
- Focus: Strategic operations, efficiency, client satisfaction
- Common Pain Points: Reporting, customization, process complexity
- Industries: Food & Beverage, Heavy Machinery, Furniture, Glass

### **Manufacturing Engineers/Managers**
- Focus: Process improvement, technical operations
- Common Pain Points: Process monitoring, data collection, technical training
- Industries: Medical Devices, Plastics, Cosmetics, Sporting Goods, Agricultural Equipment, Paper

### **Production Supervisors/Managers**
- Focus: Day-to-day production, operator guidance
- Common Pain Points: Work instructions, quality control, documentation
- Industries: Aerospace, Cosmetics, Paper
